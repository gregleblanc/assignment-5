About

Old Rules is a rock band from Ottawa, Ontario, Canada formed in February 2012. Taking influence from a range of rock-based genres like emo, grunge, alternative, Old Rules delivers a familiar sound with character. Consisting of vocalist Cody Coughlan, guitarists Nathaniel Plamondon and Holden Egan, bassist Cory Bergeron, and drumm Mat Nadeau-Lemyre, the band is working towards the release of their next recording: Blurred.

Cody Coughlan - Vocals Nathaniel Plamondon - Guitar Holden Egan - Guitar Cory Bergeron - Bass Mathieu Nadeau-Lemyre - Drums
